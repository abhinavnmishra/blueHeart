Blue Hearts


Wellness

People seek therapy when they feel blue. We reach out when our emotions become overwhelming. We may feel helpless, confused, and worthless while being stuck in a myriad of emotions. But reaching out or being in this state does not make you weak. It takes a lot of strength to be able to hold space for so many emotions and go through life. You have a strong and brave heart that is feeling blue today. We invite you to sit with us and spend time with every shade of blue that comes up. We invite you on a journey where you get to know your blue heart.

Srinithi Sridhar

Counselling Psychologist
(She/ Her)


I am a trauma-informed and queer-affirmative therapist and an EFTMR practitioner. I work with clients between the ages of 18 and 60 and my practice is currently online. My work focuses on co-creating a safe space for clients to deepen their relationships with themselves and build healthy boundaries with the people they meet. I also work with clients struggling with depression, anxiety, suicidal ideations, and grief. I use an eclectic approach to therapy. My roots lie in humanistic-existential approaches as they value safety, empathy, and authenticity. These are values that I extend to my practice as well.

Frequently Asked Questions

You can book your session by clicking here. Once you fill out the form, we will reach out to you. You will receive a mail with the intake form, payment details, and a link to book your session. Take time to read the form thoroughly. Kindly ensure that all your queries are clarified before the first session. You can make the payment and book your slot after you fill out the form. Kindly note that the booking will be confirmed only after the payment.
Every client has a unique set of experiences and goals that they hope to achieve through therapy. Healing takes place both in the session and outside. It differs based on what we do with the time we have in between and a readiness to accept change. Therefore, it can be difficult to predict the right number of sessions. However, you can always communicate your concerns with your therapist and work them out together.
Currently, I do not offer a sliding scale. My sessions are at a fixed price of Rs. 1,000 per session.
Each session is for 50 minutes.
Kindly note this is not an emergency service. I will be available from 9 am to 5 pm on weekdays. You will receive a response within 24 hours except on weekends. In case of an emergency, you can contact the helpline numbers provided below.
