About Me


I am Srinithi Sridhar (She/ Her). Like many others, I was set to be an engineer. I prepared for it with utmost dedication for 4 years, but at the last moment, something told me that it would not make me happy. It took me a lot of strength to decide and read up on other career paths and let go of the lifestyle I had spent years cultivating. I chose to then pursue subjects (dance and literature) that make me happy and build my career from there. Psychology at that time was an add-on.

However, once I began studying it, I quickly fell in love with it. Concepts that I was reading about in my books were coming to life in the conversations I had and in the stories I read. I was beginning to see the immense potential that psychology has in helping us step into more authentic versions of ourselves and extend this power and kindness to others.

As a psychologist, I hope to extend and co-create similar spaces of safety with you.
