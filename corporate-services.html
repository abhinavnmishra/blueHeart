Corporate Services


Building a career is important. Many challenges make it hard to focus and develop healthy coping skills. We may have to take care of our families, process difficult emotions, take care of our health, or be the best performer. All this is very stressful and can take a toll on our psychological health. It can be helpful to have a space where you do not perform and can express yourself freely. While these challenges may be yours to handle, you do not have to do it alone.

Employee Wellness Programs (EWPs)




EWPs are interventions that are designed for workspaces. All of us bring stress from different parts of our lives. It can be helpful for employees to have a space to unwind and work through the blocks that are affecting them at work. This can be done through workshops or one on one counselling sessions.

Counselling Sessions




Multiple sources of stress impact us. It can be hard to navigate through them. It can be helpful to reflect and introspect with a professional therapist to find helpful ways to cope with these challenges.

Workshops

• Self-care practices
