Employee Wellness Programs (EWPs)




EWPs are interventions that are designed for workspaces. All of us bring stress from different parts of our lives. It can be helpful for employees to have a space to unwind and work through the blocks that are affecting them at work. This can be through workshops or through one on one counselling sessions.

Counselling Sessions




There are multiple sources of stress that impact us. It can be hard to navigate through them. Regardless of your role in the company, it can be helpful to reflect and introspect.
