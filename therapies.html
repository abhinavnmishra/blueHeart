What is Therapy?


Therapy is a space where you can reflect, process, and work through your thoughts, emotions, behavioural patterns, and experiences in a safe manner. Therapy allows you to be yourself as you get to know different parts of who you have been, who you are, and whom you are becoming. It is different from talking to your loved ones, as here you have this full hour to only focus on your story. It gives you the space to feel heard and seen and valued for who you are. While you receive care and warmth from this space, there is also a commitment toward growth and healing at a pace that feels comfortable for you.

Whatever your reason for therapy may be would like to extend warmth and welcome you to this space.
